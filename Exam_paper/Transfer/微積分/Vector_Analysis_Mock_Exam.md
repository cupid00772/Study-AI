# 微積分模擬考卷：Vector Analysis 綜合測驗

## 測驗說明區
- **測驗時間**：100 分鐘
- **總分**：100 分 (每題 10 分)
- **範圍**：Vector Calculus (向量微積分)，特別針對**方向導數與單位向量**、**Green 定理與奇點 (Singularity)**、**面積分 (Surface Integral)** 與 **散度定理 (Divergence Theorem, 三維空間積分)** 進行強化。
- **作答建議**：請確實寫下計算過程。若有使用特殊定理，請標明定理名稱與檢查其適用條件。

---

## 題目區

**1. (Directional Derivative)**
Find the maximum rate of change of $f(x, y, z) = x^2 y^3 z^4$ at the point $P(1, -1, 1)$, and determine the **unit vector** in the direction in which this maximum rate of change occurs.

**2. (Green's Theorem with Singularity)**
Let $C$ be the ellipse $x^2 + 4y^2 = 4$ oriented counterclockwise. Evaluate the line integral:
$$ \oint_C \frac{-y}{x^2 + y^2} dx + \frac{x}{x^2 + y^2} dy $$

**3. (Surface Integral - Flux)**
Evaluate the surface integral $\iint_S \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S}$, where $\mathbf{F}(x,y,z) = x\mathbf{i} + y\mathbf{j} + z^2\mathbf{k}$, and $S$ is the part of the paraboloid $z = 1 - x^2 - y^2$ that lies above the $xy$-plane, oriented upward. 

**4. (Divergence Theorem & Spherical Coordinates)**
Let $S$ be the unit sphere $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1$ oriented outward. Use the Divergence Theorem to evaluate the flux of the vector field $\mathbf{F}(x, y, z) = x^3 \mathbf{i} + y^3 \mathbf{j} + z^3 \mathbf{k}$ across $S$.

**5. (Stokes' Theorem)**
Use Stokes' Theorem to evaluate $\oint_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r}$, where $\mathbf{F}(x, y, z) = -y^2 \mathbf{i} + x \mathbf{j} + z^2 \mathbf{k}$ and $C$ is the curve of intersection of the plane $y + z = 2$ and the cylinder $x^2 + y^2 = 1$. (Orient $C$ to be counterclockwise when viewed from above).

**6. (Conservative Vector Field)**
Determine if the vector field $\mathbf{F}(x, y, z) = (2xy + z^3)\mathbf{i} + x^2 \mathbf{j} + 3xz^2 \mathbf{k}$ is conservative. If it is, find a potential function $f$ such that $\nabla f = \mathbf{F}$, and evaluate $\int_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r}$ where $C$ is any curve from $(1, -2, 1)$ to $(3, 1, 4)$.

**7. (Green's Theorem - Standard)**
Use Green's Theorem to evaluate the line integral $\oint_C (e^x - y^3) dx + (\cos y + x^3) dy$, where $C$ is the circle $x^2 + y^2 = 9$ oriented counterclockwise.

**8. (Scalar Surface Integral)**
Evaluate the surface integral $\iint_S z dS$, where $S$ is the upper hemisphere $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 4, z \ge 0$.

**9. (Line Integral - Work)**
Find the work done by the force field $\mathbf{F}(x, y, z) = y \mathbf{i} + z \mathbf{j} + x \mathbf{k}$ in moving a particle along the twisted cubic curve $\mathbf{r}(t) = \langle t, t^2, t^3 \rangle$ from $t = 0$ to $t = 1$.

**10. (Divergence Theorem - Cylinder)**
Evaluate the flux of $\mathbf{F}(x,y,z) = x\mathbf{i} + y\mathbf{j} + z\mathbf{k}$ outward across the closed surface $S$, where $S$ is the boundary of the solid cylinder $x^2 + y^2 \le 4$, $0 \le z \le 3$.
