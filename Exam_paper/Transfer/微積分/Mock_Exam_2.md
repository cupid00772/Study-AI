## 乙、計算、證明題：共 3 大題，每大題 12 分，共 36 分。
**(須詳細寫出計算及證明過程，否則不予計分。)**

**1. (12 points)**
Consider the power series $\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(x-2)^n}{n \cdot 4^n}$.
(a) (6 points) Use the Ratio Test to find the radius of convergence of this power series.
(b) (6 points) Determine whether the series converges absolutely, converges conditionally, or diverges at the endpoints of its interval of convergence. Be sure to state any convergence tests you use.

**2. (12 points)**
Use the method of Lagrange Multipliers to find the absolute maximum and absolute minimum values of the function $f(x, y) = xy$ subject to the constraint $x^2 + 4y^2 = 16$.

**3. (12 points)**
Let a piecewise function be defined by:
$$
f(x, y) = \begin{cases} \frac{x^3 y - x y^3}{x^2 + y^2} & \text{if } (x, y) \neq (0, 0) \\ 0 & \text{if } (x, y) = (0, 0) \end{cases}
$$
(a) (6 points) Use the limit definition of partial derivatives to find $f_x(0, y)$ for all real numbers $y$, and find $f_y(x, 0)$ for all real numbers $x$.
(b) (6 points) Show that the mixed partial derivatives at the origin exist, but $f_{xy}(0,0) \neq f_{yx}(0,0)$.
