# 微積分 掃題大師 (Question Sweeper) - Chapter 7: 瑕積分 (Improper Integrals)

**測驗範圍：** 瑕積分之收斂與發散判定、計算、Type 1 (無窮區間) 與 Type 2 (瑕點/垂直漸近線)、極限比較審斂法、p-積分。
**難度：** 台聯大轉學考難度 (中偏難)
**總分：** 100 分 (共 8 題)

## 第一部分：計算題 (Evaluate the improper integrals)

**1. (10%)** Evaluate the integral or show that it is divergent:
$$ \int_{0}^{\infty} x^2 e^{-x} \, dx $$

**2. (10%)** Evaluate the integral or show that it is divergent:
$$ \int_{-1}^{1} \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \, dx $$

**3. (10%)** Evaluate the integral or show that it is divergent:
$$ \int_{0}^{3} \frac{1}{x-1} \, dx $$

**4. (15%)** Evaluate the integral or show that it is divergent:
$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{x}{1+x^2} \, dx $$
*(注意：必須嚴格遵守瑕積分定義，不可直接假設對稱性相消)*

**5. (15%)** Evaluate the integral:
$$ \int_{2}^{\infty} \frac{1}{x \ln x (\ln(\ln x))^2} \, dx $$

## 第二部分：收斂性判定 (Determine Convergence or Divergence)

**6. (10%)** Determine whether the improper integral converges or diverges:
$$ \int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{\sin^2(x)}{x^2} \, dx $$

**7. (15%)** Determine whether the improper integral converges or diverges. Use an appropriate convergence test (e.g., Limit Comparison Test):
$$ \int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{3x^2 + \sqrt{x}}{x^4 + 2x^2 + 1} \, dx $$

**8. (15%)** Determine whether the improper integral converges or diverges:
$$ \int_{0}^{1} \frac{e^{-x}}{\sqrt{x}} \, dx $$
